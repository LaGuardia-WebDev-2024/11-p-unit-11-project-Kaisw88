My Favorite Albums at the Moment

  1. channel ORANGE

    Frank Ocean's channel ORANGE has been on repeat over the past couple of months and I've grown and absolute obsession
    with this project, along with the rest of his discography. As a songwriter and producer myself, I've been taking so much
    inspiration from his encapsulating storytelling and simplistic, yet seemingly perfect production that stands as the backbone of this work

  2. Absolutely

    Dijon's debut album, absolutely has such a unique sound that stems from its live, somewhat messy recording styles and
    methods, which gives it a raw quality that sticks with the listener. Dijon and Mk.gee's production and playing make for
    such a beautifully emotional and passionate combination of sounds that I am so drawn to.

  3. Songs In The Key Of Life

    This album is considered by myself and many to be one of the greatest of all time. This album will inspire my music
    for the rest of my life with it's absolutely genius harmony writing sprawled across every track and beautiful lyrics and
    melodies that pack the piece with emotion
